Elliott from Stardew Valley

~we all saw this coming

Elliott is a character from the successful indie video game, Stardew Valley, made by Concerned Ape. He is a writer who lives in the cabin on the beach and happend to move to the valley a year befor the farmer.

In my opinion, the best way to describe him is as floppy.

In Stardew Valley all of the NPCs have loved and liked gifts, these are items that you can give the character and gain friendship with them.

Elliott's favorite gifts are:

  • Lobster which can be gotten from crabpotts in the beach
  • Squid Ink which are dropped by Squid Kids on floors 91-119 of the mines
  • Crab Cakes
  • Duck Feathers which are produced by ducks, after you get the Big Coop upgrade
  • Pomogranate which grow on pomogranate trees during Fall
  • Tom Kha Soup

Elliott's birthday is on Fall 5th. Gifts given on a characters birthday are worth three times more friendship.
